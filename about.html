About PDFMerge

The story behind the free PDF merger created by a student, for students.

The Problem

As a student, I found myself constantly needing to merge PDF files for assignments, research papers, and study materials. However, every time I searched for a PDF merger online, I was met with the same frustrating experience:

  • Free trials that required credit card information
  • Hidden fees and premium features
  • Watermarks on merged documents
  • File size limitations
  • Privacy concerns with uploading documents

I realized that many students face the same struggle - we need simple tools to help with our studies, but we don't have the budget for expensive software or subscriptions.

The Solution

That's when I decided to create PDFMerge - a completely free, privacy-focused PDF merger that students can use without any financial burden. Here's what makes it different:

  • 100% Free: No hidden fees, no premium features, no credit card required
  • Privacy First: All processing happens in your browser - files never leave your device
  • No Limitations: No file size limits, no watermarks, no restrictions
  • Student Made: Built by someone who understands student needs
  • Simple & Fast: Clean interface that gets the job done quickly
